Science Venture Online Registration System

www.scienceventure.ca/register

PHP · Javascript/jQuery · MySQL · HTML5/CSS3
Built for Science Venture
June - August 2015

The Science Venture online registration system allows users to quickly and easily register for the group's extremely popular youth STEM programs.

It features a simple account-based system that allows users to pre-enter their camper information and for a streamlined experience on registration day — many programs sell out in just minutes! It also provides powerful and flexible tools that allow program administrators to manage program information with fine-grain control.

I was responsible for the design and technical implementation of the entire system, including the database, backend, and frontend. I worked closely with program administrators and technical staff to develop and deploy the system, which now registers hundreds of campers each year.
